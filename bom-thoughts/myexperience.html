My Experience

My name is Kimball Christensen. I have grown up a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I was baptized at eight years old, and since then I have stayed active in the church. Ever since I can remember, the Book of Mormon has been taught as a significant part of my relationship with Christ. Many people are under the impression that in the Church, there is a lot of pressure to live a certain way.

I have always made the choice, however, to find the truth for myself by studying Christian texts and then asking God for His help in revealing the truth to me.
